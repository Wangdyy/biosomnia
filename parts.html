Promoters

TetR Repressible Promoter

Part: Part:BBa_R0040

  • Inverting regulator that is constitutively ON and repressed by Tet repressor protein (TetR)
  • TetR repression is inhibited by the addition of tetracycline (TC) or its analog, anhydrotetracycline (aTC)
  • Two Tet operator binding sites for TC or aTC
  • TC or aTC eliminates repression of promoter and activates transcription and expression of the target gene, caffeic acid o-methyltransferase (COMT)
