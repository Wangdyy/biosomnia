TetR repressible promoter

The TetR repressible promoter is made up of two Tet operators, both having a TetR binding site. Promoter is constitutively ON and repressed by TetR (Tet Repressor Protein). Protein TetR repression is inhibited by the addition of tetracycline or its analog. Two Tetracycline (Tc) binding sites, which when Tc binds TetR can no longer repress the promoter and Tc activates transcription/expression of the target gene.
